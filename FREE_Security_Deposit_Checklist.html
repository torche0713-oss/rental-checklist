🔑 Security Deposit Inspection Checklist

Free Download | Philippine Rental Guide

What is this? This checklist helps you document the property condition during move-in and move-out. Keep one copy at each stage to protect yourself from deposit disputes.

⚖️ RA 9653 Quick Rules:

  • Security deposit = Maximum 1 month rent (anything more is void)
  • Deposit must be kept separate from other funds
  • Must be returned within 30 days after move-out
  • Only deduct for: unpaid rent, damages beyond normal wear, breach of lease
  • Normal wear and tear CANNOT be deducted
