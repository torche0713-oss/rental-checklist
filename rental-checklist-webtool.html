Before the Lease: Legal Basis

RA 9653 (Rent Control Act of 2009)

Owner has proven legal ownership (title, tax declaration)
Property has valid Tax Declaration from Assessor
For condos: Certificate of Title available
No pending legal disputes or mortgage
Owner has valid government-issued ID
If agent: written authorization verified
2

Tenant Screening

Verify before signing

Valid government ID (PhilID, passport, driver's license)
Employment verification (COE, business permit)
Income proof (3x monthly rent requirement)
Previous landlord reference (if applicable)
Background check (credit, criminal)
Co-maker or guarantor (if required)
3

Lease Terms

Clear agreement = no disputes

Fixed-term vs month-to-month clearly stated
Monthly rent amount and due date
Payment method (cash, GCash, bank transfer)
Advance + Deposit (1 month each standard)
Grace period (3-5 days recommended)
Late fee policy (not over 5% under RA 9653)
4

Security Deposit

RA 9653 caps at 1 month rent

Maximum 1 month deposit (RA 9653)
Itemized inventory at move-in
Photo documentation of condition
Deposit held in escrow (not misused)
Deduction list for damages
Refund timeline (within 30 days)
5

Utilities & Common Areas

Clear responsibilities

Electricity usage - tenant pays
Water usage - tenant pays
Internet/Cable - tenant registers
Common area rules (pool, gym, parking)
Utility sharing (if applicable)
Guest policy - hours, overnight limits
6

Maintenance & Repairs

Know responsibilities

Landlord responsibility (structural, major)
Tenant responsibility (minor, neglect)
Reporting procedure (written, with photos)
Response timeline (24-72 hours)
Emergency contacts listed
AC, heater, appliances - maintenance plan
7

Renewal & Termination

Know the rules

Notice period (30 days minimum)
Renewal terms (60-90 days notice)
Rent increase limit (RA 9653)
Valid reasons for termination
Eviction process (court order required)
Force majeure clause
8

Move-Out & Refund

Protect your deposit

Move-out notice (30 days)
Final walkthrough scheduled
Utility transfer arranged
Keys returned - documented
Deposit refund within 30 days
Deduction receipt provided
9

Move-In Documentation

Start right

Signed lease agreement
Condition report (with photos)
Meter readings documented
Keys received - inventory
HOA registration (if condo)
Emergency contacts received
10

Airbnb / Short-Term Rental

If applicable

Building allows Airbnb - verified in writing
Minimum stay policy (if any)
Guest registration required
Platform rules (Airbnb, Booking.com)
Cleaning between guests
Tax remittance (BIR, local)
