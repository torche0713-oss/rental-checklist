🏢 Condo for Airbnb? Read This First

Essential Checklist Before You Buy

💡 Many buyers think: "I'll buy a condo, list it on Airbnb, and earn monthly income." But not all condos allow short-term rentals. Check this first!

🚩 COMMON PROBLEM

  • You buy the unit for Airbnb
  • Then management says: Short-term stays not allowed
  • Minimum lease is 6 months or 1 year
  • Guests must be registered with strict limits
  • Penalties for repeated violations

🏢 WHY SOME CONDOS RESTRICT AIRBNB

  • Building management protects security of residents
  • Maintains peace and privacy for homeowners
  • Reduces elevator/common area wear and tear
  • Limits frequent stranger access
  • Maintains community standards

    • 🧾 WHAT TO CHECK BEFORE BUYING

  • House Rules / Condo Policies
  • Master Deed restrictions
  • Leasing guidelines
  • Guest registration policies
  • Minimum rental term allowed
  • Short-term vs long-term rental rules
  • Penalties for violations
  • Written confirmation received

    • ⚠️ IMPORTANT

    Don't rely on verbal promises. Get written confirmation if possible. Real estate agents may not know all building rules - verify yourself!

    💰 WHY THIS MATTERS

  • If Airbnb not allowed, income projections may fail
  • ROI may slow down significantly
  • You may need long-term tenants instead
  • Vacancy risk changes completely
  • Exit strategy may be affected

    • 🔥 SMART BUYER MOVE

    If your goal is rental income, choose condos that are:

    • Investor-friendly
    • In high-demand locations
    • Clear on leasing policies
    • Good for both Airbnb and long-term rental

    🧠 BEST ADVICE

  • Don't buy a condo based only on location or price
  • Buy based on RULES
  • Buy based on DEMAND
  • Buy based on CASH FLOW
  • Buy based on EXIT STRATEGY

    • 💡 REMEMBER

    A condo can be a great investment... but only if your rental plan is actually allowed!
